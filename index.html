What assembly club is ?

Assembly Club was founded in September 2022 by a two Saudi students dedicated to sharing their passion with others. Based in Saudi Arabia, we are an active and enthusiastic Programming Club with events and activities taking place week-round. We like to keep up the energy and excitement, always welcoming new members to take part. Simply put, we love getting together to do what we love.
